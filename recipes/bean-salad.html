Bean Salad with Balsamic Vinaigrette

Description

Any combination of cooked beans will work in this protein packed salad. It is actually better the next day. Make sure the red onion is finely chopped.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz can chickpeas
  • 16 oz can light red kidney beans
  • 2/3 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
  • 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • salt and ground pepper

Steps

  1. In a small bowl combine parsley, olive oil, vinegar, basil, sugar, salt and pepper
  2. Mix well.
  3. Place all ingredients in medium bowl and toss well to combine.
  4. Cover and refridgerate for at least one hour.
    5. Makes 4 servings
