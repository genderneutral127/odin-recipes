Greek-style Israeli Couscous Salad

Description

Greek rendition of Lebanese tabbouleh. This salad gives a kick of tangy feta and salty kalamata olives. For a vegan dish use vegan feta. Make sure to allow the flavors time to meld. Feel free to adjust herbs and spices to your personal preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup Israeli couscous dry
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 15 oz can chickpeas drained and rinsed
  • 15 pitted kalamata olives sliced
  • 3 oz feta cheese or vegan feta cheese
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatoes sliced
  • Optional washed raime lettuce leaves for serving

Steps

  1. In a medium sauce pan over medium heat bring broth, onion powder, and garlic powder to a boil.
  2. Boil on low heat with lid on for 10 minutes. Turn of stove.
  3. add 2 tablespoons of lemon juice to pot, stirring into couscous. Leave lid on pot to steam coucous 5 minutes. Coucouc will absorb all remainin broth
  4. In a large bowl whisk oil, vingar, oregano, salt and pepper
  5. Add in chickpeas, olives and feta. Stir to combine
  6. Add couscous to bowl, gently mixing ingredients
  7. salt and pepper to taste
  8. refriderator minimum of two hours in fridge. Optionally serve on a bed of lettuce.
    9. Makes 4 main dish servings, makes 8 side servings
