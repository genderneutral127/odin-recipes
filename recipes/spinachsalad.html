Spinach Salad with Oranges

Description

This colorful salad is ideal to serve during the bleak winter months. Argula can replace the spinach for an extra peppery punch. Makes an excellent side salad for your main entree. Give the red onions at least 10 minutes with the salt for a more mellow flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 10 oz bag fresh baby spinach
  • 2 large naval oranges, peeled and segmented
  • 1/2 small red onion, very thinly sliced and sprinkled with salt
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts toasted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, orange juice, salt and pepper.
  2. Add the spinach, orange segments, red onion and pine nuts.
  3. Toss gently and thoroughly combine.
  4. Serve at once.
    5. Makes 4 servings
